#### Details pane
Selecting any item will display additional information on the **Details Pane** where you can also perform other actions quickly.

#### Context menu
Right-clicking on an item or any empty space in the browser will present you with additional actions for that context.

## 🧭 Navigation

Clustta has a unique navigation system where to view or access the contents of a collection(folder), you can simply click on the **chevron** icon to the left of the collection to expose its contents. This way, you can have an overview of everything in a simple file tree view without going folders deep just to access files.

<div align="center">
  <img src="/images/the-browser/exposed-collection.png" alt="Description">
</div>
<br>

Double-clicking on a collection will isolate the contents in the view and act as a normal browser.

<div align="center">
  <img src="/images/the-browser/navigator-mode.png" alt="Description">
</div>
<br>

You can go up a level or jump to any location using the **Breadcrumbs** bar.

<div align="center">
  <img src="/images/the-browser/breadcrumbs-bar.png" alt="Description">
</div>
<br>
